For a fixed input difference to round compute a list of output differences that satisfy the following conditions:

  1. The probability of the differential is bigger than a pre-defined threshold p_thres .
  2. The input difference to the next round has a matching entry in the pre-computed pDDT hways_diff_set_dx_dy.
