Transition probability matrices A.

A[2][2][2] = A[a[i]][db[i]][dc[i]], where

  • a[i] : the i-th bit of the fixed input.
  • db[i] : the i-th bit of the input difference.
  • dc[i] : the i-th bit of the output difference.
